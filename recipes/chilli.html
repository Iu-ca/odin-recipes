Famous Chilli

Description

This famous chilli recipe is the Texas-style chilli con carne, which is known for its bold and robust flavor. This recipe typically includes cubed beef chuck, onions, garlic, chilli powder, cumin, beef broth, tomatoes, and sometimes even chocolate or coffee if you're insane.

The chilli is simmered for hours until the meat is tender and the flavors have melded together. It is often served with toppings such as shredded cheese, sour cream, and green onions. Texas-style chilli is a classic comfort food and has become a staple in many households and restaurants across the United States.

Ingredients

  • Beef chuck or Plant-based substitute
  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Chilli powder
  • Garlic
  • Beef stock

Steps

  1. Dice your vegetables and equip a mid sized pan
  2. Add oil to the pan and begin cooking your veg
  3. Add a clove of garlic and a tbsp of chilli powder
  4. Add your beef and cook until browned and mixed into smaller chunks
  5. Mix beef stock with 300ml of water, mix and add to the pan
  6. Simmmer for a bit until done, anywhere from 15 minutes to 4 hours usually does it
  7. Cook your rice by boiling water just above where your rice rests in the pan - once this has boiled through your rice is ready to add
