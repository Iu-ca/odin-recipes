Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs are a simple dish made by whisking together eggs, salt, and pepper and cooking them in a pan until they are fully cooked and slightly fluffy. They are a popular breakfast food and can be customized with a variety of additions such as cheese, herbs, or vegetables. Scrambled eggs are a versatile dish that can be served on their own, as part of a breakfast sandwich, or alongside other breakfast foods like bacon or toast.

It has often been asked by the world's leading scientists how can we create the best scrambled egg in existence? Look no further than this recipe, as I'm something of a scientist myself.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk together 2 eggs, salt and pepper.
  2. Add to a medium sized pan and fry on a medium heat
  3. Stir until the eggs solidify, serve when at the desired texture or after 5 minutes
